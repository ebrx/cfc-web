隐私政策 · Privacy Policy

最后更新 / Last updated: 2026-06-20

CFC 用你设备的摄像头扫描附近屏幕上的 cimbar 动态彩色码，在本机还原出文件。本政策说明它处理与不处理哪些数据。

CFC uses your device camera to scan animated cimbar color barcodes shown on a nearby screen and reconstruct the encoded file on-device. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

文件如何传输How Your File Is Transferred

发送端屏幕把文件编码成彩色码，接收端用摄像头读取并解码。文件数据只走「屏幕 → 镜头」这条光学链路，端对端直接传输，全程不经过任何服务器——不上传、不中转、不缓存、不存盘。

The sender's screen encodes your file as color barcodes; the receiver's camera reads and decodes them. The file data travels only over the optical "screen → lens" link — end-to-end, directly, never through any server: nothing is uploaded, relayed, cached, or stored remotely.

我们收集的数据Data We Collect

没有。CFC 不收集、不传输、不出售、不共享任何个人数据。没有账号、没有统计分析、没有广告、没有第三方追踪。

None. CFC collects, transmits, sells, and shares no personal data. No accounts, no analytics, no ads, no third-party tracking.

摄像头Camera

摄像头权限仅用于读取 cimbar 彩色码。画面帧完全在本机内存中处理用于解码，不录制、不保存到相册、不上传。

Camera access is used solely to read cimbar barcodes. Frames are processed entirely on-device, in memory, and are not recorded, saved to your photo library, or uploaded.

接收到的文件Files You Receive

传输完成后，还原出的文件保存在你的设备本地。除非你自己选择转发或分享，它不会离开设备。我们无法访问。

When a transfer completes, the reconstructed file is saved locally on your device. It never leaves the device unless you move or share it yourself. We have no access to it.

网络Network

打开本网页需要网络（页面托管在 GitHub Pages）。但解码过程本身不使用任何网络连接。唯一的外部跳转是你点击「发送」时打开公开网站 https://cimbar.org 以编码文件，该网站受其自身条款约束。

Loading this page requires a network connection (it is hosted on GitHub Pages). However, the decoding process itself uses no network connection. The only external navigation is when you tap "Send," which opens the public site https://cimbar.org to encode a file; that site is governed by its own terms.

儿童Children

本应用不面向儿童，也不向任何人收集数据。

This app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.
