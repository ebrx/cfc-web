Privacy Policy — CFC

Last updated: 2026-06-19

CFC ("the app") is a tool that uses your device camera to scan animated cimbar color barcodes shown on a nearby screen and reconstruct the file they encode. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

Data We Collect

None. CFC does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

Camera

The app requires camera access solely to read cimbar barcodes. Camera frames are processed entirely on your device, in memory, to detect the code and decode it. Frames are not recorded, saved to your photo library, or uploaded to any server.

Files You Receive

When a transfer completes, the reconstructed file is saved locally inside the app's own Documents folder on your device. These files never leave your device unless you choose to move or share them yourself (for example, through the Files app). We have no access to them.

Network

The decoding process uses no network connection. The only time the app may open a network resource is if you tap the "Send" button, which opens the public website https://cimbar.org in your browser so you can encode a file. That is an external website governed by its own terms.

Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
